About me

I'm a video editor, photographer, and multimedia creative who enjoys turning an idea into something people actually stop and watch. My work spans social content, event coverage, interviews, and promotional video — always aiming for clean storytelling, tight pacing, and a finished product that looks professional.

Day to day, I work mainly in Adobe Premiere Pro, DaVinci Resolve, Photoshop, and Lightroom, taking projects from concept through to final delivery. Beyond editing, I've operated broadcast cameras for international summits, managed livestreams, and built and run a company website from the ground up.

Whether I'm working solo or alongside a client's team, I care about the same three things: creativity, professionalism, and getting the small details right.
